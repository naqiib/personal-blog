My Educational Journey

Author - Naqib
16 November | 4 min read

My journey in education has been an exciting adventure that shaped the person I am today. I started my academic career in my hometown, Chitral, where I completed my FSC. During those years, I developed a strong foundation in science and mathematics, which eventually inspired my passion for technology.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Air University. I am in my 4th semester and have gained extensive knowledge in programming, database management, and software development. My curriculum also emphasizes critical thinking and problem-solving skills, which have been instrumental in my academic and professional growth.

Aside from my studies, I have worked on several projects that blend theoretical knowledge with practical application. These projects include a web application for the online sale, purchase, and exchange of books, along with designing user-friendly interfaces using tools like Blazor WebAssembly. My experience with SQL and MongoDB has added depth to my skill set.

Education is not just about academic achievements; it is also about the experiences and lessons learned along the way. I am excited to continue this journey, constantly exploring and growing in the field of computer science.
